MA5kinG ver. 1.9 -- MASkinG fo Allegro5
-- by Milan Mimica, developed on top of MASkinG v0.80 by Miran Amon.

Use cmake to build. Requires Allegro5 with iio (zlib, png), kcm_audio, font, ttf
(TrueType) and acodec (vorbisfile, vorbis, ogg) addons.
